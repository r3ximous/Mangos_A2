Delicious Mango Recipes

Mango Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe mango, peeled and chopped
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions:

  • Combine all ingredients in a blender.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • Pour into a glass and enjoy!

Mango Salsa

  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  • In a bowl, combine all ingredients.
  • Mix well and let sit for 10 minutes to allow flavors to meld.
  • Serve with tortilla chips or as a topping for grilled fish or chicken.

Mango Sorbet

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe mangos, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Instructions:

  • In a saucepan, combine sugar and water. Heat until sugar is dissolved.
  • Let syrup cool.
  • In a blender, combine mango, syrup, and lemon juice. Blend until smooth.
  • Pour mixture into a shallow dish and freeze until firm.
  • Scrape with a fork to create a sorbet texture and serve.
