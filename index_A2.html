All About Mangos

Mangos are tropical stone fruits that belong to the genus Mangifera. They are native to South Asia, specifically India and Myanmar, but are now grown in many tropical and subtropical regions around the world.

Geography of Mangos

Mangos thrive in warm climates and are primarily grown in countries like India, China, Thailand, Indonesia, and the Philippines. They are also cultivated in parts of Africa, South America, and the Caribbean.
