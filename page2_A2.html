Rich in Nutrients

Mangos are packed with vitamins and minerals, including vitamin C, vitamin A, and folate. They are also a good source of dietary fiber and antioxidants.

Boosts Immunity

The high vitamin C content in mangos helps boost the immune system, protecting the body against infections and diseases.

Improves Digestion

Mangos contain enzymes that aid in digestion and help break down proteins. The fiber content also promotes healthy digestion and prevents constipation.

Promotes Eye Health

Vitamin A in mangos is essential for maintaining good vision and preventing night blindness and dry eyes.
